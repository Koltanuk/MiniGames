How to Play

Bulls and Cows is a classic code-breaking game. The objective is to guess a 4-digit secret number where each digit is unique.

Rules:

  • Enter a 4-digit number with unique digits.
  • After each guess, you will receive feedback in the form of "bulls" and "cows".
  • Bull: A digit is correct and in the right position.
  • Cow: A digit is correct but in the wrong position.
  • Use the feedback to guess the correct number.
